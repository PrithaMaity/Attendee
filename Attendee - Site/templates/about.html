About ATTENDEE

ATTENDEE is a project that is started by us because taking attendance is a very tedious task in our day to day life and storing this attendance is not that easy task. So we came up with a new idea to make attendance easier and faster and taking record of the attendance is also so easy task

As we have started working on this project since November'22 and we have started this as a college project. And we have implemented this project in a virtual machine. In this project we are mainly using better quality cameras for recording the attendance. And we have made portals for one for students and and one for faculty members and staff members. Where all the students,faculty and staff members have their own unique id which is been given by us.

How the attendance is being recorded ?

Our attendance recording process is as simple as from pen-paper attendance system. In our system images of all the students, faculty and staff members are saved with a unique id given from our side and they have to simply stand in front of our camera and our systems compares your current image with saved images in our system and the attendance is been marked. And here students can check their today's attendance is marked or not in the students portal. And faculty members can see the recorded attendance of students at any time.
